MUSCLE CRAMPS

Symptoms

  • Sudden Pain
  • The muscle affected by the cramp may feel tight, hard, or visibly contracted.
  • Painful Sensations
  • Muscle Fatigue

SOLUTION

  • Gently stretch the affected muscle to help alleviate the cramp. Hold the stretch for about 15-30 seconds.
  • Gently massage the cramped muscle to help relax it.
  • Apply heat or cold to the cramped muscle.

SOLUTION FOR MENSTRUAL CRAMPS

  • Apply heat to the lower abdomen using a hot water bottle, heating pad, or warm towel.
  • Taking a warm bath can help relax your muscles and provide relief from menstrual cramps.

HEADACHE

Symptoms

  • Slow onset of the headache
  • Head usually hurts on both sides.
  • Pain is dull or feels like a band or vice around the head
  • Pain may involve the back part of the head or neck.

SOLUTION

  • Rest
  • Cold or warm compress
  • Stay hydrated

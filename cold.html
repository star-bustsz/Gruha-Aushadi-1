COLD

Symptoms

  • A blocked or runny nose
  • Sore throat
  • Headaches
  • Sneezing
  • Pressure in your ears and face

SOLUTION

  • Honey in water
  • Steam
  • Lemon water
  • Body needs rest

SOLUTION FOR BLOCKED NOSE

  • Place a warm, wet towel on your face.
  • breathe in steam from a pot of warm (but not too hot) water

SOLUTION FOR RUNNY NOSE

  • Resting.
  • Drinking plenty of fluids, especially water.
  • Inhaling steam two to four times a day.
